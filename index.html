Fuel Your Performance: 8 Healthy Recipes for Dancers & Athletes

Balanced meals for energy, flexibility, and recovery

1. Power Smoothie Bowl

Purpose: Boosts energy and hydrates muscles pre-workout.

  • 1 frozen banana
  • ½ cup spinach
  • ½ cup Greek yogurt
  • ½ cup almond milk
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 tbsp almond butter
  • ¼ cup mixed berries

Blend all ingredients and top with berries and almond butter.

2. Quinoa & Roasted Veggie Bowl

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 cup roasted sweet potatoes
  • ½ cup chickpeas
  • ½ cup broccoli florets
  • 2 tbsp olive oil, 1 tsp paprika

Roast veggies, mix with quinoa, and drizzle with olive oil or tahini.

3. Protein Energy Bites

  • 1 cup oats
  • ½ cup peanut butter
  • ⅓ cup honey
  • ¼ cup dark chocolate chips
  • 1 scoop protein powder

Mix ingredients, roll into balls, and refrigerate.

4. Grilled Salmon with Brown Rice & Asparagus

  • 1 salmon fillet
  • ½ cup brown rice
  • 1 cup asparagus
  • 1 tbsp olive oil, lemon juice, salt & pepper

Grill salmon and serve with rice and sautéed asparagus.

5. Overnight Oats with Berries

  • ½ cup oats
  • ½ cup almond milk
  • ½ cup Greek yogurt
  • ½ cup mixed berries
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds, 1 tsp honey

Mix and refrigerate overnight; enjoy cold or warm.

6. Chicken Wrap with Avocado

  • 1 whole wheat tortilla
  • ½ grilled chicken breast
  • ¼ avocado
  • ½ cup spinach
  • 1 tbsp hummus or Greek yogurt

Spread hummus, add ingredients, roll up, and slice.

7. Greek Yogurt Parfait

  • ½ cup Greek yogurt
  • ¼ cup granola
  • ¼ cup berries
  • 1 tsp honey

Layer ingredients in a glass or jar and drizzle with honey.

8. Stir-Fry Tofu with Brown Rice

  • ½ block tofu
  • 1 cup mixed veggies
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • ½ cup brown rice

Sauté tofu and veggies, add soy sauce, serve over rice.

Performance Tips

  • Hydrate: 2.5–3L of water daily.
  • Pre-Workout: Carbs + light protein.
  • Post-Workout: Protein + complex carbs.
  • Prep ahead to stay consistent.
